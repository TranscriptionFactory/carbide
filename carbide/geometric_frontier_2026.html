KL-Batching

A storage-level heuristic that partitions graphs using clustering algorithms. Batches are aggregated to minimize the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence between the batch's edge-type distribution and the global graph. This ensures every batch is a statistically representative microcosm of the network.

Round-Robin Batching

A GPU-level heuristic that prevents "hyper-prevalent" edge types from overwhelming gradient updates. Edges are sequestered by type and fed in a cyclic sequence, preventing catastrophic forgetting of rare structural relations.

Agentic GraphRAG: The R1 Paradigm

Graph Retrieval-Augmented Generation has evolved from static lookups to agentic reinforcement learning. The **R1 Series** (AutoGraph, GraphRAG, ProGraph) integrates outcome-based and process-constrained rewards to optimize both the construction and navigation of knowledge graphs.

AutoGraph-R1

Target: KG Construction Policy

Task-Aware

Uses Knowledge-Carrying Rewards ($R_C$) to incentivize structurally contiguous pathways required for multi-hop deduction. Prevents decoupled, suboptimal graph creation.

GraphRAG-R1

Target: Reasoning Policy (GRPO)

Cost-Aware

Implements Progressive Retrieval Attenuation (PRA) to force deep-graph exploration, while a Cost-Aware F1 (CAF) reward penalizes computational over-thinking.

ProGraph-R1

Target: Step-Level Progress

Structure-First

Provides dense, intermediate rewards based on uncertainty reduction at every node hop. Uses hypergraph retrieval to enforce actual topological connectivity over hallucinated semantic similarity.

Resolving MPNN Pathologies

Message Passing Neural Networks (MPNNs) face the dual threats of **Oversmoothing** (loss of distinction) and **Oversquashing** (information bottlenecks). This section details the mathematically rigorous solvers introduced in late 2025 and early 2026.

Spectral Sparsification

Selectively rewires graphs to alleviate squashing bottlenecks while strictly preserving the eigenvalues of the graph Laplacian, maintaining the topological fingerprint.

Fractal Shortcuts

Injects hierarchical hub connections that reduce effective resistance between distant nodes, enabling long-range propagation without hyper-connectivity inflation.

Riemannian Solvers

Models message-passing as a Heat Equation solver on a Riemannian manifold. Locally adjusts boundary conditions at each node to mitigate squashing without causing gradient vanishing.
