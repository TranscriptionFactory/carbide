The parity test (`mcp_mutating_parity.rs`) correctly detects a pre-existing divergence:

- **Rust**: `reindex` tool has `mutating: true` in `src-tauri/src/features/mcp/tools/search.rs:75`
- **TS**: `reindex` is NOT listed in `MUTATING_MCP_TOOLS` in `src/lib/features/rag/domain/agent_file_ops.ts`

The test FAILS on this divergence by design. Per PROMPT "Do NOT" instructions: "if they already diverge, STOP and record in STATUS.md — do not silently fix." The test is correctly implemented and proven to catch divergence in both directions (verified with a temporary bogus entry).

**Impact:** The test cannot be green until this divergence is resolved by a human decision. The `reindex` tool rebuilds the search index — it does mutate vault state (triggers re-indexing), so `mutating: true` on the Rust side appears intentional. The TS side may need `"reindex"` added to `MUTATING_MCP_TOOLS`.

**Resolution requires:** Supervisor decision on whether to add `reindex` to TS `MUTATING_MCP_TOOLS` or remove `mutating: true` from the Rust side.
